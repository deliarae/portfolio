About


Hi, my name is Delia! I was born and raised in Switzerland and, growing up, I have always wanted more than the slow, small-town life. So in 2017 I packed my bags, sold most of my possessions and booked a one-way ticket to South America, where I did a 2-week crash course in Spanish, and then travelled the continent by myself.

In June 2018 I came to London. Since then I have been working in the Hospitality industry, which has been a real learning adventure. I began as a member of the bar team and worked my way up to becoming the Bar Assistant Manager. This journey has taught me some important skills like good communication, being quick on my feet to find solutions to challenges, and working smoothly in a team.

Last year, I decided to step away from management level to focus on my personal growth and my future. While exploring different paths, I took an online course to learn the basics of Web Development, and that was what sparked my interest in this field. Since then, I've been learning from the Odin Project and Codecademy. In May, I started an intensive bootcamp where I got a solid handle on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, SQL, and Python. I'm aware there is still a lot to learn, but I'm eager to expand my skills and knowledge, and I'm always seeking opportunities to grow.
